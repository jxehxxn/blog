## Prompt
"Walk me through a project you're proud of."

## 답 구조
1. Problem.
2. Approach options.
3. Choice + trade-off.
4. Implementation 핵심.

## 흔한 follow-up
- Why X over Y?
- What would you do differently?
- How did you measure success?
