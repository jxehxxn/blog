## 5 Tactics
1. Anchor high.
2. Multi-dimensional (base/bonus/equity).
3. Time pressure 피함.
4. Email 협상 (생각할 시간).
5. Walk away ready.

## Counter offer
"I really love the team. To make this work, can we get base up to X and sign-on to Y?"

## Equity 협상
hardest. refresh 추가 요구.

## 빅테크 average increase
협상 후 10~30% 추가.

## 결론
협상 안 하면 평생 손실 (compounding).
