## Pre-interview
- 30 system design 패턴 학습 (Designing Data-Intensive Applications).
- 5+ 실제 design 연습.

## During
1. Clarify (5min).
2. Estimate (3min).
3. API (5min).
4. High-level (10min).
5. Detail (15min).
6. Scale + failure (10min).
7. Questions + summary (5min).

## Common pitfalls
- Jumping to solution.
- Ignoring non-functional req.
- 모름 hide.

## 결론
framework + 시간 관리.
